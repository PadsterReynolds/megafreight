Marine Insurance

Megafreight is a registered provider of marine insurance (FSP No.17723) where we offer peace of mind cover to suit most requirements under our own marine policy re-insured through reputable companies such as LLoyds of London. The service is supported by many of our clients meaning we have remarkable bulk buying power and flexibility. Premiums are charged at shipment level allowing for all-inclusive and instant view of your cost per shipment while reducing your number of suppliers and removing the expense from your income statement.
